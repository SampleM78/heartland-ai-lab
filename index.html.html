Section 2 · 5–22 min
Who can AI become for the Heartland team?
AI isn't one thing. It's a different partner depending on what you need in the moment — and it's available 24/7, never in a meeting, and never too busy.
The many roles of AI
AI is whoever you need right now.
Each role below is something AI can genuinely do for your work — not theoretical, not future-state. Today.
Thought Partner
When you're stuck on how to approach a problem, AI helps you think it through — surfacing angles you hadn't considered, challenging assumptions, stress-testing ideas before you commit to them.
"Help me think through how to structure this GMP conversation with a client who is already frustrated."
Brainstorm Partner
Need five ways to frame a risk, ten agenda ideas for a kickoff, or three approaches to scope reduction? AI generates options fast so you can evaluate, not invent.
"Give me 5 different ways I could position a 3-month schedule delay to a client who is already concerned."
First Draft Creator
The hardest part of writing is starting. AI drafts in seconds — emails, reports, meeting minutes, proposals — giving you something to react to and improve rather than a blank page to fill.
"Draft a weekly status report from these bullet points. Format for a non-technical client."
Review Copilot
Paste a document, an email, or a proposal and ask AI to check it — for tone, gaps, jargon, missing information, or whether it actually answers the client's question.
"Review this client email. Does it bury the lead? Is the ask clear? What would a client think when they read it?"
Simplifier / Translator
Construction and project management are full of jargon that confuses owners. AI rewrites technical content into plain language instantly — for board decks, client summaries, or executive updates.
"Rewrite this for a CFO who has no construction background but needs to approve a budget amendment."
Research Assistant
Need a quick summary of a contract section, a comparison of two approaches, or background on an unfamiliar building system? AI synthesizes and summarizes so you don't have to read everything.
"Summarize the key risks in this owner-contractor agreement language. Flag anything unusual."
Professor / Explainer
When you need to understand something quickly — a new regulation, an unfamiliar construction method, a financial concept — AI explains it at exactly the level you need, as many times as it takes.
"Explain embodied carbon requirements in plain terms. I need to brief a client who is asking about sustainability."
Template Builder
Describe a document you create repeatedly and AI builds a reusable template with fill-in-the-blank sections — meeting agendas, status reports, RFP responses, handover checklists.
"Build a weekly owner's representative status report template I can reuse every Friday in under 5 minutes."
Rehearsal Partner
Preparing for a difficult conversation, a board presentation, or a client challenge? AI plays the other side — pushes back, asks the hard questions, and helps you prepare better answers.
"Play the role of a skeptical client who is about to push back on our GMP overrun. I'll practice my response."
AI is excellent at
  • >Drafting, rewriting, summarizing text
  • >Structuring messy notes and data
  • >Generating options and alternatives
  • >Translating complexity into plain language
  • >Building reusable templates and frameworks

What still needs you

Section 3 · 22–36 min
The only framework you need.
Four letters. Every great prompt has all four. Learn it in 2 minutes. Use it forever.
C
CONTEXT
Give it your real situation
"Project: Heartland, $52M GMP, steel pricing 12% over. Here are my raw notes: [paste]"
O
OBJECTIVE
Tell it exactly what to do
"Write professional meeting minutes. Include decisions, action items with owners and due dates."
R
ROLE
Tell it who to be
"You are a senior JLL project manager specializing in commercial construction..."
E
EXAMPLE
Show it what good looks like
"Format exactly like this: [paste an example or describe the structure you want]"
Works in JLL GPT today. Works in Claude when it arrives. Works in every AI tool forever. Learn this once — it never expires.
